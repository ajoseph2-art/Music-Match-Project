About MusicMatch

MusicMatch is a social music discovery platform that connects you with communities of music lovers who share your taste and helps you discover new music tailored to your preferences.

Features

Community Groups

Connect with communities based on your favorite genres and discover music through shared interests.

Listening Parties

Join live listening rooms with your community members and experience music together in real-time.

Collaborative Playlists

Create and contribute to community playlists where everyone can add their favorite tracks.

Reviews & Ratings

Share your thoughts on songs and albums with the community through reviews and ratings.

Music Matching

See your compatibility score with other users based on your music taste and preferences.

Smart Recommendations

Get personalized recommendations based on activity mode, time of day, and song attributes like danceability.

How It Works

  1. Connect your Spotify account to import your playlists and favorite songs
  2. Join communities that match your music taste
  3. Discover new music through community playlists and recommendations
  4. Participate in listening parties and collaborative playlists
  5. Share reviews and connect with users who have similar music preferences
